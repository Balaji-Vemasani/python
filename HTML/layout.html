Sentimental Analysis has various applications. It is used to generate opinions for people of social media by analyzing their feelings or thoughts which they provide in form of text. Sentiment Analysis is domain centered, i.e., results of one domain cannot be applied to another domain. Sentimental Analysis is used in many real-life scenarios,to get reviews about any product or movies, to get the financial report of any company, for predictions or marketing.

Amazon is a micro blogging platform where anyone can read or write short form of message which is called tweets. The amount of data accumulated on amazon is very huge. This data is unstructured and written in natural language. Amazon Sentimental Analysis is the process of accessing tweets for a particular topic and predicts the sentiment of these tweets as positive, negative or neutral with the help of different machine learning algorithm.
